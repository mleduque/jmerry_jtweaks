Level: Quest
Range: 0
Duration: Permanent until dismissed
Casting Time: 1
Area of Effect: The caster
Saving Throw: None

Harnessing the power of fire, this powerful shapechange ability allows the Druid to become a 24-HD fire elemental of immense strength. The elemental form has an AC of -5, a THAC0 of 2, and attacks once per round for 1d10 crushing and 1d10 fire damage, striking as a +4 weapon. When the Druid returns to human form, <PRO_HESHE> is also healed 3d10 Hit Points.
